About Anirudh Ravichander

Anirudh Ravichander is one of the most popular music directors in India. He made his debut with the song "Why This Kolaveri Di" from the movie 3 (2012), which became a viral hit.

Over the years, he has composed music for blockbuster films like Vikram, Jailer, Leo, and many more. His music is known for its energetic beats and unique style.

Awards: SIIMA, Filmfare, National Award, and many more.
